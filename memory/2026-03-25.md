## Key decisions
- Source validation should reconcile the derived package to `../jobads-data/main/data/processed/20*/processed_*.parquet`, not just to `metadata.json`.
- The dashboard should fail validation when source totals drift or when critical schema columns disappear from `monthly_by_market.parquet` or `monthly_skills_topk.parquet`.
- The skills table should store detailed local aggregates and let the app compute top-k after filtering, rather than materializing a heavier multi-scope grouped top-k cube during refresh.
- Industry panels should describe only postings with usable industry codes; the synthetic `Unknown industry group` stays out of those mix charts and tables.
